## Series

### In this set, we will discuss some of the sets avaiable.
 1. Series 1- The LEGO CMF  Series 1 was released in 2010 and includes 16 unique minifigures such as the Zombie, Circus Clown, and Forestman. These figures came in a polybag with accessories and a stand This is the first cmf set that Lego came out with.
 2. Series 10- LEGO CMF 10 was released in May 2013 and includes 16 minifigures, plus the rare Mr. Gold, of which only 5,000 were made. This figure is currently worth 8000.00 dollars.
 3. Series 5- LEGO CMF Series 5 was released in August 2011 and includes 16 minifigures. Notable characters in this series are Lizard Man, Evil Dwarf, Gladiator, Egyptian Queen, and Royal Guard.
As you can tell, these are a wide variety of figures offered by lego.
